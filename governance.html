Governance Structure

Assembly of Parties, Standing Committee, President’s Office and Secretariat

Organisational Structure

Assembly of Parties (Plenary)

  • Approve Annual Budgets
  • Decide Fund Allocation
  • Admission / Removal (2/3 majority)
  • Normal decisions: Double-majority voting

Standing Committee (Executive)

  • Operational decisions between sessions
  • Recommend the President
  • Supervise the Secretariat
  • Major financial decisions (2/3 majority)

President’s Office

  • Principal Executive Official
  • Public face of the Bank
  • Final executive decisions

Secretariat

Handles day-to-day work of the Bank under supervision of the Standing Committee.
