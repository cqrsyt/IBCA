Funding Sources

Capital structure designed for sustainability and scale

1. Member State Capital

  • GDP-based paid-in capital
  • Callable capital (rarely used)

2. Capital Market Borrowing

  • Issue climate bonds
  • Maintain strong credit rating

3. Co-financing Partnerships

  • Multilateral Development Banks
  • Climate funds

4. Project Revenue (Revolving)

  • Loan repayments
  • Guarantee fees

The funding model combines traditional paid-in capital from member states with market borrowing capacity and partnerships, creating a revolving and scalable resource base dedicated solely to climate adaptation projects.
