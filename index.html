Why a Dedicated Institution?

Existing international financial institutions and climate funds have inadequately addressed the urgent adaptation needs of the most vulnerable. Extreme weather, sea-level rise and ecosystem degradation disproportionately threaten Least Developed Countries and Small Island Developing States.

IBCA fills this critical gap with a narrow, legally binding mandate that prevents mission creep while delivering focused, accountable support.
