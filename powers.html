Privileges and Immunities

The Bank, its assets, premises and official communications shall be inviolable and immune from every form of legal process, tax or interference, except insofar as it has expressly waived its immunity. Officials enjoy immunity for acts performed in their official capacity.

Legal Processes

Jurisdictional immunity for bank loan decisions.

Assets & Property

Complete protection from financial freezing or seizure.

Premises & Archives

Absolute inviolability against host state intrusion.

Fiscal Status

Tax exemption for all institutional income and property.

Personnel

Staff immunity from personal civil/criminal liability.

Communications

Freedom of unhindered contact with member states.
